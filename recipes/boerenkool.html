Boerenkool

Description

Boerenkool is a typically Dutch dish. It is created by cooking kale and potatoes. During the cooking, the kale and potatoes are mashed together. Optionally, the boerenkool can be enriched with small pieces of bacon. Last but not least, adding chunks of sausage completes it!

It is not difficult to make. Follow the recipe and you will have your own amazing meal in no time!

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs potatoes
  • 1 large white onion
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 lb curly kale
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 pinch ground pepper
  • 1 lb smoked sausage
  • 1 ‚ÅÑ2 cup milk
  • 2 Tablespoons butter

Steps

  1. Peel and dice potatoes and onion.
  2. Clean, trim and slice kale.
  3. Add the potatoes, onion, kale, a bay leaf, a pinch of salt and just enough water to cover all in a pool.
  4. Cover and boil gently for about 25 minutes.
  5. Meanwhile steam or grill the smoked sausage until done then slice into bite size pieces.
  6. Remove the bay leaf, drain the vegetables, and mash them.
  7. Add milk and butter.
  8. Add in sliced smoked sausage.
  9. Add salt and pepper to taste and serve.
