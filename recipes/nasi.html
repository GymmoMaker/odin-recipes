Nasi Goreng

Description

Nasi Goreng is one of the most delicious dishes in the world. It consists of baked rice with pieces of (chicken) meat and pieces of cooked vegetables, as well as some nasi mix materials. This results in a warm and rich collection of foods.

It is not difficult to make. Follow the recipe and you will have your own amazing meal in no time!


  • 4 Gloves of Garlic
  • 1 Medium Onion, Sliced
  • 4 Red Chilies, cut into 3
  • 1 Inch of Ginger, sliced
  • 1 Cup of Dried Anchovies
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Cup of Frozen Peas
  • 1 Teaspoon of Salt
  • 1 Tablespoon of Light Soy Sauce OR Liquid Aminos
  • 3-4 Cups of Cooked Rice
  • 4-6 Tablespoons of Cooking Oil
